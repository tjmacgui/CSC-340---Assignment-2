Our Menu

Welcome to Elmo's Scrumptious Sandwiches! Your favorite place for delicious sandwiches made with love!

Happy Ham & Cheese

A mouth-watering combination of honey-baked ham, melted cheddar cheese, fresh lettuce, and heirloom tomatoes on a toasted whole-grain bun.

Joyful Turkey Delight

Oven-roasted turkey breast, creamy avocado spread, crispy bacon, swiss cheese, and mixed greens on a soft ciabatta roll.

Cheerful Chicken Club

Grilled chicken breast, crisp bacon, ripe tomatoes, crunchy lettuce, and a tangy ranch dressing on toasted sourdough bread.

Veggie Paradise

A medley of fresh veggies including cucumbers, bell peppers, spinach, red onions, and hummus, topped with feta cheese and served on a multigrain roll.

Elmo’s PB & J

Classic peanut butter and jelly sandwich, made with creamy peanut butter and your choice of grape or strawberry jelly, served on soft white bread.
